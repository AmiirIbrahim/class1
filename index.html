ABOUT US

Oman Air (WY), based in the beautiful capital city of Muscat, began operations in 1993. Initially founded to serve important domestic routes, we have since undergone rapid growth and today, we are proud to be recognised as a major international carrier connecting cities around the world to Oman’s stunning nature, rich heritage and welcoming culture. With a mission to highlight Oman as one of the Middle East’s most desirable travel destinations, while supporting adjacent commercial, industrial and tourism activities, we continue to expand our impact and our global customer-base.
